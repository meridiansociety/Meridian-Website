About

A social and intellectual space for the globally curious.

The Meridian Society is a student-run, Ottawa-based speaker forum that brings together curious students, alumni, and professionals to share experiences, perspectives, and pathways across travel, careers, and public life.


Through interactive presentations, open conversations and informal gatherings, the Society helps its members orient themselves in an increasingly complex world, and gain exposure to people and opportunities they would not otherwise encounter.

Our Name

What is a Meridian?

In geography, a meridian is a line used to orient the world.

We liked the idea of creating a place that helps people orient themselves, not by telling them where to go, but by exposing them to different paths.

The Meridian Society exists to do something simple and intentional: bring the right people into the same room - to listen, to learn, and to ask better questions.

What We Are Not

Honest about what we don't offer.

The Meridian Society is not for everyone, and that's intentional. We won't pretend to be something we're not.

Not a resume workshop
Not a career placement office
Not a political advocacy group
Not a startup incubator
Not a closed elite circle

We cannot promise outcomes.
We can only provide access, exposure, and orientation to people and their ideas.

Speaking at The Meridian Society

Interested in sharing your experience with our community?

The Meridian Society regularly hosts presentations, open conversations and informal gatherings where professionals, alumni, and thinkers share their experiences and perspectives. If you have a story, insight, or path worth sharing, we want to hear about it.

We would love to hear from you.

Reach out to us directly at meridiansocietycanada@gmail.com and tell us a little about yourself and what you'd like to share.
